Cookies

Chewy chocolate chip cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups unsalted butter
  • 3/4 packed light or dark brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Whisk ingredients together. Set aside.
  2. Cover dough tightly and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 325°C.
  4. Scoop chilled cookie dough into portions.
  5. Bake cookies for 13-14 minutes.
  6. Store tightly covered at room temperature for up to 1 week.
