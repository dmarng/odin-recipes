Brownie

Chewy fudgy frosted brownies!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate bar
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Combine butter and chopped chocolate. Melt in 30-second increments and whisk together.
  3. Add cocoa powder, flour, salt, remaining chocolate. Fold together.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and place on wire rack.
  6. Remove from oven. Cover and store.
