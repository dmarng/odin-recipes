Homemade Bread

Homemade artisan bread is only composed of 4 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 3 and 1/4 cups bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 and 1/2 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons coarse salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk together flour, yeast and salt.
  2. Cover dough tightly with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and set on the counter at room temperature
  3. Turn cold dough out onto work surface and shape into 2 long loaves
  4. Bake
